About Us

Pennie Burke is a dedicated hospice nurse who has been in direct patient care for over 12 years. As a registered nurse with a decade of experience, she has developed a deep understanding of the needs of disabled and elderly residents, and has honed her skills in providing specialized hospice care for the past 5 years. Throughout her career, Pennie has shown a remarkable ability to connect with residents and their families, helping to ease the burden of end-of-life care and support them through difficult times.


Pennie (pictured on the right) is also a loving daughter and mother of two children, her family is an important source of strength and inspiration for her.


Pennie is committed to making a positive impact on the lives of her residents. She brings a unique combination of medical expertise and empathy to her role as a hospice nurse, and is known for her ability to provide comfort and support to those in need. Her passion for nursing and her commitment to the people she helps are a testament to her unwavering dedication and professionalism in the face of difficult circumstances.
