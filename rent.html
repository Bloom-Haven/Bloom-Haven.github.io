What Does the Rent at Bloom Haven Include?

  • Room and Board with 24 hour supervision for safety

  • Care of Resident including assistance with ambulation, bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting and eating

  • Medication Management

  • **Weekly Care and updates on resident status from a Registered Nurse

  • All meals and snacks, including modified dietary needs such as altered texture foods and thickened liquids

  • **Written records of intake of food and fluids at every meal

  • **Any specialized utensils to assist with independence when eating

  • Laundry service of all personal clothing and linens

  • **All Sheets, towels and blankets provided, additional linens from home are welcome

  • **Briefs/Pull ups, gloves, wipes and other incontinence supplies

  • **Written records of bowel function and brief changes

  • Daily assistance with and general maintenance of dentures, and hearing aides.

  • **Monthly and as needed hair trimming.

  • **Monthly gentle ear Cleaning/flushing with physician approval.

  • Daily gentle exercise opportunities, and **gentle range of motion for non-mobile residents

  • Scheduled activities including music, dance, art, cooking and puzzles

  • Monthly Holiday and Birthday celebrations.

  • **Weather permitting outdoor activities such as gardening and outdoor meals.

**These items/services are typically NOT offered at assisted or independent living facilities
