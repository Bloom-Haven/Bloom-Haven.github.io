Best Items to Bring for the new Move to Bloom Haven

Medical Items

(Bloom Haven already has an electronic lift, slings, and shower chair if those are needed by resident)

The following items should be sent 1 day prior to move in date if needed.

  • Hospital bed
  • Oxygen concentrator

Bring with the resident on day of move:

  • Wheelchair
  • Walker
  • All prescribed medications and a medication list
  • Any current incontinence supplies

Clothing

  • Elastic waist pants - 14 pairs
  • Long sleeve shirts - 14 shirts (button down is better if possible)
  • Button up sweaters or zip up sweatshirts - 3
  • Long loose socks - 14 pairs
  • Pajama top/undershirt - 7
  • Comfortable slippers - 1
  • Pair of shoes - 1

Personal Items

  • Family pictures and personal decorations
  • Favorite bed pillow
  • Toothbrush and toothpaste
  • (If dentures) bring denture cup, and cleaning tabs
  • Hearing Aids w Extra batteries
  • Lotion
  • Razors and shave cream if used

Furniture

Feel free to bring what ever will fit within the room within reason but Bloom Haven provides a bed, night stand, lamp, chair, shelves and dressers

*These are suggestions for loved ones and bringing more is fine
